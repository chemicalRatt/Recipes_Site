Pizza

Ingredients

  1. Dough
  2. Sauce
  3. Cheese
  4. Pepporoni Slices

Steps

  1. Prepare your dough.
  2. Spread sauch over dough surface to liking.
  3. Spinkle cheese all over the sauce on top of the dough.
  4. Add toppings (in this case, pepporonis!)
  5. Preheat oven to whatever degrees.
  6. Cook pizza for ten minutes.
  7. Enjoy!
