Ratt's Favorite Hotdog

Hotdog - an old school (lazy) favorite.

It's plain old bread, processed meat, condiment, and happiness. What more could someone want?!

Ingredients

  • Bun
  • Dog
  • Ketchup/Mustard

Steps

  1. 1. Prepare your bun.
  2. 2. Heat up your dog, either in the microwave or boiled on the stovetop in some water.
  3. 3. Place your heated dog in the bun.
  4. 4. Lather your dog in ketchup/mustard.
  5. 5. Enjoy!
