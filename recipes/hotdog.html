Glizzy

Ingredients

  1. Bun
  2. Raw Glizzy
  3. Ketchup/Mustard

Steps

  1. Prepare your bun.
  2. Heat up your raw glizzy, either in the microwave or boiled on the stovetop in some water.
  3. Place your heated glizzy in the bun.
  4. Lather your glizzy in ketchup/mustard.
  5. Enjoy!
